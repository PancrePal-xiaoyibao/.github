## 🚀 Project Introduction

OpenCare Community & its sub-community "PancrePal" is an AI public welfare platform for cancer patients. We are committed to utilizing cutting-edge AI technology, based on the "Shared Decision Management" framework and the community's own ACT (AI-Community-Toolkits), to build a trustworthy "AI + Humanity" community. We provide accessible professional care and information support for patients and their families, **bridging the information gap and lighting the hope for life.**

## ✨ NEW: Community Driven, Glimmer to Greatness

After **36** months of iteration and evolution, PancrePal has received strong support from the **Tiangong Kaiwu Foundation** on the cornerstone of open source. We firmly believe that the power of open source can gather more wisdom and make technology truly serve people.

## 📜 [Project Open Source Principles and Oath]

Using the community project open-source framework, including the technology framework, product framework, operational framework, and knowledge base data based on this project in whole or in part, automatically accepts the following principles and oaths by default:

1. **Never charge patients and their families:** Reject "technology medical shills" and do not make money from patients through "intelligent assistants."
2. **Respect the ownership of knowledge contributors:** Do not profit through the knowledge base and disclose knowledge base sources.
3. **Everything is based on "Standardized Treatment":** "Evidence-based" is the first principle for knowledge base admission, eliminating so-called "miracle doctor" schemes.

**We welcome you to Fork and replicate this project's open-source framework, establish more OpenCares, help patients and their families, and accelerate the application of AI tools. We look forward to your active submission of PRs and Issue feedback! We even more welcome your project donations and authorizations, allowing more patients to see and use the results of excellent developers!**
